Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa powder.
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted.
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup (adjust to taste).
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • A pinch of salt.

Baking Instructions.

  1. Sift the cocoa powder into a mixing bowl to ensure a smooth texture.
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the cocoa powder and stir until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the melted coconut oil,
    honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  4. Stir the wet ingredients thoroughly until they form a homogeneous mixture.
  5. Stir the wet ingredients thoroughly until they form a homogeneous mixture.
  6. Use a spatula or whisk to combine the mixtures thoroughly, ensuring there are no lumps.
  7. Line a mold or a tray with parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  8. Pour the chocolate mixture into the mold, spreading it evenly to achieve your desired thickness.
  9. Place the mold in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours,
    allowing the chocolate to solidify and set properly.
  10. Once the chocolate has set, remove the mold from the refrigerator.
  11. Carefully lift the chocolate from the mold and place it on a clean surface.
  12. Use a sharp knife to cut the chocolate into bars or desired shapes.
  13. Feel free to customize your chocolate bars by adding nuts, dried fruits, or spices to the mixture before pouring it into the mold.
  14. Store the chocolate bars in an airtight container in the refrigerator to maintain freshness.
  15. Now, you're ready to savor your delicious chocolate bars.
    Enjoy the process and the sweet results!
